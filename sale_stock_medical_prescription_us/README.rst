====================================
Medical Prescription Sale Stock - US
====================================

Extends Medical Prescription Sale Stock with U.S-based pharmacy concepts such
remaining qty available for dispenses, remaining days available, etc.

Also adds NDCs to procurements, and RX thresholds to determine when new procurement
orders should be made.
